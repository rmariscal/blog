Summary

I’m Rodrigo Mariscal a mexican economist that has worked at the Institute of International Finance, the IMF’s Western Hemisphere Department, the Inter-American Development Bank, and Mexico’s Central Bank. I hold an M.A. in economics from El Colegio de Mexico. My research focuses on macroeconomics, with emphasis on the credibility of inflation regimes in Latin America, sovereign debt restructurings, and commodity prices.
